Tiny Pets

Tiny Pets is the perfect game for people who love animals and the gamble of a blind draw! In this game you are able to collect various different types of eggs which all contain unique and adorable creatures.

Buy Different Types of Eggs!

Three different types of eggs are available for purchase: Common, Rare, and Legendary. Each type has its own unique set of adorable creatures waiting to be hatched!

Save up coins!

As you play Tiny Pets, you'll earn coins that can be used to purchase new eggs and other in-game items. Save up your coins to get the best eggs and expand your collection!

Click! Click! Click!

All you have to do to hatch new eggs is tap the screen and watch as the egg cracks open to reveal your new pet!

About Us

Established in a Unity Hall classroom, four students, Nick Driscoll, Eliza Hackler, Matthew Papesh, and Emma Herman, came together to create a fun and engaging game that allows players to collect and care for their very own virtual pets. Sometimes we all can feel a bit lonely, but with Tiny Pets, you'll never be alone. Just peak into your pocket and see your new pets!
